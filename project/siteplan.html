Lakay Haitian Kitchen

Site Name

Lakay Haitian Kitchen

The word "Lakay" means "home" in Haitian Creole. This name reflects the welcoming atmosphere of a family-owned Haitian restaurant where guests can enjoy authentic cuisine and experience Haitian culture.

Site Purpose

This website will showcase a fictional family-owned Haitian restaurant. It will include pages for the restaurant's menu, featured dishes, locations and hours, a photo gallery of meals and the dining area, online reservation information, and customer reviews. The site may also include interactive features such as a contact form and menu filters.

Scenarios

  • What traditional Haitian dishes are available, and which meals are the restaurant's specialties?
  • How can I make a reservation, and what are the restaurant's hours and location?

Color Scheme

  • #0047AB – Headings and section titles
  • #D21034 – Highlights and accents
  • #FFFFFF – Background color
  • #333333 – Body text

Typography

Merriweather – Used for headings

Lato – Used for body text

Why I Chose This Topic

I chose this subject because one of my goals is to open a Haitian restaurant in Utah. Restaurants benefit from attractive and informative websites that showcase their food and services. This project allows me to practice HTML, CSS, and JavaScript while building a realistic restaurant website.
